At MN, we measure success in human terms. Our ultimate goal is to protect the most fundamental things that nature provides to all of us: our food, our fresh water, our livelihoods and a stable climate.

Mission

Building upon a strong foundation of science, partnership and field demonstration, MN empowers societies to responsibly and sustainably care for nature, our global biodiversity, for the well-being of humanity.

Vision

We imagine a healthy, prosperous world in which societies are forever committed to caring for and valuing nature, for the long-term benefit of people and all life on Earth.
